Welcome to Annachi Restaurant

The True Taste of Authentic South

Indian Cuisine

Exquisite Dining at Its Best

Step into the heart of South India with Annachi Restaurant, where every dish tells a story. Our passion for flavour and culture is evident in every dish, prepared with the utmost care and attention to detail. From the rich spices to the bold flavours, our cuisine is a perfect taste that will leave you satisfied. Our menu is a fusion of classic dishes and modern twists, using only the freshest ingredients and spices sourced from India. Whether you’re a food lover, or simply looking for a unique dining experience, Annachi Restaurant is the place to be.Come visit us at Annachi Restaurant and savour the true taste of Chettinadu Cuisine and our exclusive foods that are made with passion and pride.

Modern Dining

Elevated Experience

We’re more than just a restaurant – we’re an experience. Our menu features an innovative fusion of flavours and styles that draw inspiration from cuisines and cultures from around the world. Our dedicated and experienced staff are passionate about providing excellent service and creating an atmosphere that is welcoming and inclusive. Our warm and welcoming atmosphere is a reflection of our values of hospitality and inclusivity. Annachi Restaurant, we are honoured to carry on our legacy of authentic South and North Indian cuisine and share our passion for food with our community. Come and experience Modern Cuisine for yourself, and let us take you on a journey of culinary delight.
